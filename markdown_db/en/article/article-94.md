# Article 94: Procedural Rights of Economic Operators of the General-Purpose AI Model

Date of entry into force:

See here for a[full implementation timeline](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/implementation-timeline/).

#### Summary

This article states that the rules from Article 18 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1020 also apply to those who provide general-purpose AI models. This means they have certain rights when it comes to procedures. However, this doesn't affect any other specific procedural rights that are mentioned elsewhere in this regulation.
