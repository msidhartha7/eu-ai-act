# Article 39: Conformity Assessment Bodies of Third Countries

Date of entry into force:

2 August 2025

According to:

Inherited from:

See here for a[full implementation timeline](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/implementation-timeline/).

#### Summary

This section says that organizations from countries outside the EU, which have an agreement with the EU, can be allowed to perform the duties of notified bodies under this regulation. However, they must either meet the requirements set out in Article 31 or ensure they follow rules that are equivalent. In simpler terms, these organizations can do the job, but they must follow the same or similar rules as EU organizations.
