# Article 113: Entry into Force and Application

Date of entry into force:

Immediately

See here for a[full implementation timeline](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/implementation-timeline/).

#### Summary

This article states that the regulation will become effective 20 days after it's published in the Official Journal of the European Union. Different parts of the regulation will be applied at different times: Chapters I and II will be applied six months after the regulation comes into effect, certain sections will be applied after 12 months, and Article 6(1) will be applied after 36 months. The regulation is binding and directly applicable in all EU member states.

This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.

It shall apply from 2 August 2026.

However:

(a) Chapters[I](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/chapter/1/)and[II](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/chapter/2/)shall apply from 2 February 2025;

(b)[Chapter III Section 4](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/section/3-4/),[Chapter V](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/chapter/5),[Chapter VII](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/chapter/7)and[Chapter XII](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/chapter/12)and[Article 78](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/article/78)shall apply from 2 August 2025, with the exception of[Article 101](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/article/101);

(c)[Article 6](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/article/6)(1) and the corresponding obligations in this Regulation shall apply from 2 August 2027.
