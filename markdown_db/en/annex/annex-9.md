# Annex IX: Information to be Submitted upon the Registration of High-Risk AI Systems Listed in Annex III in Relation to Testing in Real World Conditions in Accordance with Article 60

#### Summary

Before any high-risk AI system is tested in real-world conditions, it must be registered according to Article 60.This Annex explains what information needs to be provided when registering high-risk AI systems for real-world testing. This includes a unique identification number for the test, contact details of the provider and those involved in the testing, a brief description of the AI system and its purpose, a summary of the testing plan, and information on any suspension or termination of the testing. This information must be kept up to date.

The following information shall be provided and thereafter kept up to date with regard to testing in real world conditions to be registered in accordance with[Article 60](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/article/60):

1. A Union-wide unique single identification number of the testing in real world conditions;

2. The name and contact details of the provider or prospective provider and of the deployers involved in the testing in real world conditions;

3. A brief description of the AI system, its intended purpose, and other information necessary for the identification of the system;

4. A summary of the main characteristics of the plan for testing in real world conditions;

5. Information on the suspension or termination of the testing in real world conditions.
